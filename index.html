About Us

At our core, we are a group of passionate individuals who share a common goal of creating a better world. Our team is comprised of volunteers who bring a diverse range of skills, experiences, and backgrounds to the table. We work tirelessly to create and implement programs that address the most pressing issues facing our community, including poverty, education, healthcare, and environmental sustainability.

Why are we doing this?

Sport can bring communities together, it can help inculcate discipline and drive passion in young people and it can help bring pride, joy and fun to everyone. Football and netball are popular activities among school-going youth. And our organisation believes that young people who participate in sport tend to do well in their studies
